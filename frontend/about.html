Biography

Sct. Aloysius O. Eluu is the visionary Founder and Director of Agapelove Medical Laboratory and Diagnostic Ltd (AMLAD). With a deep commitment to advancing healthcare, he has led the organization in providing cutting-edge diagnostics and patient-centered care across Abakaliki and Ebonyi State.
