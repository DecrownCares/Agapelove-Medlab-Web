1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) by websites you visit. They help improve functionality, remember your preferences, and enhance your browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a. Necessary Cookies

These cookies are vital for our website’s operation and security. They allow core functions such as account logins and session management.

  • Authentication cookies
  • Session cookies

b. Performance Cookies

These cookies gather data on how visitors use our website, helping us identify areas to improve.

c. Functionality Cookies

They enable enhanced personalization by remembering user preferences such as language settings.

d. Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies deliver relevant ads and track ad campaign performance.

e. Third-Party Cookies

We allow trusted third parties (analytics providers, advertisers) to set cookies for enhanced services.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Enable/Disable cookies via browser settings
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Opt out of specific cookie categories (e.g., advertising cookies)

Browser Instructions:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy & Services > Cookies and site permissions
