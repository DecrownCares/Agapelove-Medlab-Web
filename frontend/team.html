Biography

Sct. Aloysius O. Eluu is the visionary Founder and Director of Agapelove Medical Laboratory and Diagnostic Ltd (AMLAD). With a deep commitment to advancing healthcare, he has led the organization in providing cutting-edge diagnostics and patient-centered care across Abakaliki and Ebonyi State.

Driven by a passion for healthcare excellence, Sct. Eluu has spearheaded initiatives that have positioned AMLAD as one of the top diagnostic centers in the region. His dedication to quality, precision, and trust has earned him the respect of both patients and the medical community alike.
